WELCOME TO MY WEBZONE

Hello, I'm Sal, also known as Mystery, and this is my webzone. This spot is the very spot where I keep all of my internet shit. I'm an originally-from-Brazil, now based in Newark, NJ & Brooklyn, NY based programmer, artist, and games maker. In my spare time, I enjoy actually playing video games and board games, watching streams on twitch.tv, eating food, petting cute animals, not being in a mod cabal, and enjoying TV, music, and movies. I'm happily employed as a junior full stack developer at Priori Legal.

As a Programmer

I'm a programming languages nerd. Currently, I'm interested in functional programming languages, like Clojure/script, Haskell, Elm, and F#, and Rust (which is not quite functional, but has a lot of) very excellent language design inspired by functional languages. I also like to code in Javascript, Ruby, and C. The tech I'm currently working with include Ruby on Rails, Angular.js, Heroku, Git/hub professionally, and Arcadia, Unity, React.js, Om, Flux/us on the hobby side. Currently I'm exploring the applying Rich Hickey's "Easy is not Simple" speech to game development. Game dev tools cater to rich studios that can handle complexity in exchange for performance, or to non-technically oriented individuals, who need an easy programming environment. I believe game development tools that aim to reduce the complexities of development would be a great boon to the indie scene, and would spur the growth of new and interesting gameplay mechanics.

As an Artist

I'm more of a programmer than an artist, but I've worked on a few art projects. The majority of my art work is game-oriented. I'm a huge proponent of utilizing gameplay to create art in game&emdash;hence my interest in providing simple, powerful tools to small time indies. Largely, I've only had the chance to work on small practice games, but I'm working with a team to possibly produce something cool by the end of 2015.
